About Me

A UI/UX Designer with over 4 years of experience driven by a genuine passion for solving complex problems and transforming them into simple elegant and accessible user experiences. I believe that great design begins with a deep understanding of user needs (empathy) and ends with a solution that not only meets those needs but exceeds them. My process is rooted in User-Centered Design starting from research and competitive analysis moving through wireframing and interactive prototyping and finishing with usability testing to ensure the best possible outcome. I enjoy collaborating with cross-functional teams from product managers to developers to build a bridge between technical goals business objectives and user expectations. When I'm not busy designing I enjoy reading books on behavioral psychology exploring new coffee shops in the city and practicing photography
